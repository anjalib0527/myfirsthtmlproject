Our Signature Recipes

1. Cheeseburger Casserole

Craving the flavors of a juicy cheeseburger but in a hearty, comforting form? Our Cheeseburger Casserole combines all the best parts of a classic burger—ground beef, melted cheese, tangy pickles, and a rich tomato sauce—layered with pasta and baked to perfection. It's an easy, satisfying dish that the whole family will love.

2. Chopped Cheese

Get a taste of New York's iconic street food with our Chopped Cheese recipe. This sandwich features seasoned ground beef, gooey melted cheese, and a mix of onions, peppers, and tomatoes, all chopped together on a flat-top grill and served on a toasted hoagie roll. It's a quick, flavorful meal that brings the best of the city to your kitchen.

3. Creamy Southern Lima Beans

Discover the creamy, buttery delight of our Creamy Lima Beans. This dish elevates the humble lima bean with a rich, velvety sauce made from cream, garlic, and a hint of nutmeg. Perfect as a side dish or a vegetarian main course, it’s comfort food at its finest, offering a taste of southern hospitality with every bite.
